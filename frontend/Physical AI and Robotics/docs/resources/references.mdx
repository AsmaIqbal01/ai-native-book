## Books and Textbooks

### Robotics Fundamentals

- **Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control** by Kevin Lynch and Frank Park
  Free version: [modernrobotics.org](http://modernrobotics.org/)

- **Probabilistic Robotics** by Sebastian Thrun, Wolfram Burgard, Dieter Fox

- **Introduction to Autonomous Mobile Robots** by Roland Siegwart

## Getting Help

If you're stuck, try these resources in order:

1. **Check documentation** - Most issues are covered in official docs
2. **Search forums** - ROS Discourse and Stack Overflow
3. **GitHub issues** - Project-specific problems
4. **Ask the community** - Post detailed questions on forums
5. **Debug systematically** - Use `ros2 doctor`, logs, and visualizations
